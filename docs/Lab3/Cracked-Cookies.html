Description:

These traditional Christmas cookies are a cut above the rest because they’re as rich and fudgy as brownies. They’re thick and soft-baked in the centers with a little extra chocolate for good measure!

As classic as Christmas sugar cookies and as irresistible as peanut butter blossoms, these chocolate crinkles will outshine every other cookie on your Christmas cookie platter. And that’s a guarantee

Ingredients:

  • 300 gr. Flour
  • 85 / 70 gr. Cocoa
  • 400 / 300 gr. Sugar
  • 0.5 cups of sunflower oil
  • 4 Chicken egg
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 0.5 cups of powdered sugar
  • Little bit of Vanillin
  • Little bit of Salt

Directions:

  • Beat eggs with sugar
  • Beat it with Cocoa
  • Beat it with salt, vanilla and oil
  • Add flour and baking powder. (The dough turns out soft, but hardens a little in the refrigerator)
  • Refrigerate overnight
  • Bake at 180 degrees for 12 minutes
  • Sprinkle with powdered sugar
