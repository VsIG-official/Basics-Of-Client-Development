The ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken eggs
  • 100 gr. of Butter
  • 100 gr. of Sugar
  • 250 gr. of Flour
  • 1 tsp. of Baking powder
  • Half tsp. of Vanillin

The process:

  • Beat eggs with sugar and vanilla
  • Add oils and baking powder
  • Whisk
  • Add Flour and Milk
  • Whisk
  • Pour everything into the waffle iron
