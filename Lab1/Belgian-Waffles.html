Ingredients:

  • 2 Chicken eggs
  • 100 gr. Butter
  • 100 gr. Sugar
  • 250 gr. Flour
  • 100 ml. Milk
  • 1 tsp. Baking powder
  • Half tsp. Vanillin

Directions:

  • Beat eggs with sugar and vanilla
  • Add oils and baking powder
  • Whisk
  • Add Flour and Milk
  • Whisk
  • Pour everything into the waffle iron
